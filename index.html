Modern Still life
Jan Martin Sigua
Art Review
generated in September 30, 2024
Still lifes prompt us to reflect on the essence of everyday objects, transforming the mundane into the profound. Sigua’s "Modern Still Life" exemplifies this, using a minimalist approach to mirror contemporary design trends.

The composition features a palette dominated by cool reds and neutral tones, evoking a sense of emotional detachment often found in digital work environments. Yet, the subtle pops of color within the objects symbolize vitality amidst the seriousness of modern life.

This piece highlights the tension between technology reliance and the importance of self-care (1). By focusing on a few essential items—like a laptop stand alongside a traditional notebook—Sigua emphasizes the necessity of balancing digital and analogue creativity in today’s fast-paced world.

Ultimately, this still life, while minimal, is rich in meaning, offering a snapshot of modern student life that underscores the themes of balance, health, and efficiency in a digital age (2).

Portrait of a Student
Jan Martin Sigua
generated in September 30, 2024
Growing up in a close-knit family of six in the Philippines, Sigua developed a passion for the arts and design at an early age. He began creating comics, board games, and characters, a pursuit that continued through his studies at Monash University. For his CDS3002 class, Sigua crafted this still life as a reflection of his beliefs regarding generative AI technology. Embracing a minimalist lifestyle, he expresses a lack of material desires, as evidenced by his limited purchases over the past two months. Additionally, the presence of a vitamin bottle suggests a focus on health, though it may also hint at an awareness of his well-being. Through his work, Sigua invites viewers to consider the intersection of technology, minimalism, and personal health in contemporary life.
